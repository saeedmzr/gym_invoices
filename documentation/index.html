Getting Started

Introduction

This is a simple script that helps you manage your user's check-ins and clubs.



How it works

First, you have an admin user that can manage clubs, users, and their memberships.

After you, you create your clubs and users and assign memberships to them. Users can log in to your app and then check-in to one of your clubs. the script will check their membership status then they can check-in successfully.
